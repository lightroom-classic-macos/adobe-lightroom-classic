## Tips for Safer Use ⚠️

- Keep Lightroom **disconnected from Adobe servers** after patching.
- **Back up your catalog** weekly (File > Export as Catalog).
- Use **smart previews** if working from an external drive.
- Disable auto-updates in Creative Cloud (if installed).
- Store original RAW files on a separate drive from the catalog.

## FAQ ❓

**Is this the full version?**  
Yes. All modules (Library, Develop, Map, Book, Slideshow, Print, Web) are unlocked.

**Does it require a Creative Cloud subscription?**  
No. The pre-activated version works entirely offline without payment.

**Can I sync with Lightroom mobile?**  
No — sync is disabled intentionally. Use local network transfer or AirDrop.

**Will my old presets work?**  
Yes. Copy your `.lrtemplate` or `.xmp` files into the Settings folder.
